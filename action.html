How Can Your Actions Help?

The best thing you can do to slow down the progress of climate change is to reduce the amount of greenhouse gasses your activities cause.

Move the sliders around to see how reducing certain activities reduces your carbon footprint. Then pledge to try and reduce your carbon footprint by that amount


Turning Down The Heat and A/C

Cooling is even more power hungry than heating. If you need to stay cool, use a lower power alternative like a fan.

Using Less Water

It takes a lot of energy to process and move water



Reduce How Much Trash You Throw Away

Everything you used and threw away took a lot of energy to create. Trash sitting in a landfill also makes greenhouse gasses.

Walk or Bike When Travelling Locally

It's good exercise too.

Replace Your Lightbulbs with LEDs

You use help reduce your footprint and save on your electric bill.

This will reduce your carbon footprint by pounds of CO2 a year

Change can come in small steps. You don't need to completely change your life to do these things. Just remember that every single action counts.
Set some values you can commit to and print out pledge to reduce your carbon footprint!
